About Us

At Laundry Lauds, we offer specialized reverse logistics services in returns management for retailers and manufacturers. Our expertise includes handling customer exchanges, refunds, and processing returned products, whether they are defective, damaged, or simply no longer needed. Additionally, we provide commercial laundry solutions, including scheduled pickup services for emergency services, government facilities, and large businesses that generate high volumes of laundry.

Company History: Laundry Lauds is a locally-founded and innovative company, established with the vision to reshape the textile industry. We take pride in delivering high-quality laundry services that cater to the evolving needs of our clients and the environment. Our commitment is to offer not just services, but exceptional solutions that exceed our clients' expectations.

Mission Statement: Our mission is to lead in innovation and excellence within the textile industry by offering services that are both durable and modern. Client satisfaction and environmental stewardship are at the core of everything we do.

Kanyisile Magudu, Founder and CEO
